Requirements for joining Colorado's Elusive Ingredient

  • Must be 18 or older
  • Must be available for most home shows (last Saturday of the month)
  • Must be available for most cast meetings (second Saturday of the month)
  • Must read and agree to follow the cast rules
  • Must have reliable internet access
  • Must have reliable transportation to/from shows and meetings (typically located in Denver)
  • Must be willing and able to acquire/make own costumes

Things you should know before applying

  • All cast applicants are volunteers. This is not a paid job.
  • We are not actors/a theater group. This is a shadow cast which strives for a balance of professionalism and fun.
  • Photos will be taken at shows and posted online.
